Application Amount

As an Eligible Retail Unitholder you are entitled to acquire 1 New Unit for every 3 existing fully paid Units held on the Record Date, being 7.00pm (Sydney time) on Friday, 12 October 2018 (Entitlements), at an Offer Price of AU$2.43 per New Unit outlined below. Eligible Retail Unitholders who take up their full Entitlement may also apply for Additional New Units in excess of their Entitlement up to 50% of their full Entitlement (to the extent available) at the Offer Price of AU$2.43 per New Unit. In the event of oversubscriptions, the allocation of Additional New Units will be subject to scale back on a pro-rata basis. There is no guarantee that you will be allocated any Additional New Units.

If you are in doubt as to how to deal with this online Application, please contact your accountant, solicitor, stockbroker or other personal adviser without delay. The Retail Offer Booklet contains information relevant to a decision to invest in New Units and you should read the entire Retail Offer Booklet carefully before applying for New Units. Defined terms in the Retail Offer Booklet have the same meaning as in this online Application.

If you have any questions about the Retail Entitlement Offer, you should consult your stockbroker, solicitor, accountant or other professional adviser. You can also contact the Centuria Metropolitan REIT Investor Relations Line on 1800 182 257 (within Australia) or +612 9290 2689 (outside Australia) at any time from 9.00am to 5.00pm (Sydney time) Monday to Friday, during the the period of the Retail Entitlement Offer.
